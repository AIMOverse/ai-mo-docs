## Incentive Program

We believe in rewarding our community members who actively contribute to the growth and success of AiMo Network. Our incentive program recognizes different types of contributions and provides meaningful rewards.

### Community Roles

#### Code Contributor
Contributors who enhance our codebase through various means while following our code of conduct:
- Submit issues and bug reports
- Develop new features and improvements
- Participate in technical discussions
- Review pull requests and provide feedback

#### Content Creator
Community members who amplify AiMo's reach through content creation and social media promotion:
- Produce educational content about AiMo
- Share updates and insights on Twitter/X
- Create tutorials and guides on Reddit
- Write blog posts and technical articles

#### Community Moderator
Active participants who help maintain and grow our Discord community:
- Assist community members with questions
- Moderate discussions and maintain community standards
- Organize community events and initiatives
- Welcome new members and facilitate onboarding

### Incentives & Rewards

#### Free Credits
Regular airdrops of platform credits based on contribution level and activity:
- **Monthly distributions** for consistent contributors
- **Bonus rewards** for exceptional contributions
- **Milestone rewards** for significant achievements

#### Core Contributor Recognition
Outstanding contributors may be invited to join our Core Contributor program:
- **Special Discord roles** and recognition
- **Early access** to new features and updates
- **Direct communication** with the development team
- **Governance participation** in community decisions

#### Future Airdrop Eligibility
Active community members will be prioritized for future token distributions:
- **Contribution tracking** for fair allocation
- **Long-term commitment** rewards
- **Multi-dimensional scoring** based on various contribution types

## Code of Conduct

Our community follows these principles:
- **Be respectful** - Treat everyone with kindness and respect
- **Be collaborative** - Share knowledge and help others learn
- **Be professional** - Maintain high standards in all interactions
